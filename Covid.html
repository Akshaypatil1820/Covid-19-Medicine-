Covid-19 Precautions


Precautions

  1. If you have minor symptoms, such as a slight cough or a mild fever, there is generally no need to seek medical care. Stay at home, self-isolate and monitor your symptoms.Follow national guidance on self-isolation.
  2. However, if you live in an area with malaria or dengue fever it is important that you do not ignore symptoms of fever. Seek medical help.When you attend the health facility wear a mask if possible,keep at least 1 metre distance from other people and do not touch surfaces with your hands.If it is a child who is sick help the child stick to this advice.
  3. Seek immediate medical care if you have difficulty breathing or pain/pressure in the chest. If possible,call your health care provider in advance, so he/she can direct you to the right health facility.
